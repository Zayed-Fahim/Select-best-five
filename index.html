Paris Saint Germain

Paris Saint-Germain Football Club commonly referred to as Paris Saint-Germain,
Paris, Paris SG or simply PSG is a professional football club based in Paris, France.
They compete in Ligue 1, the top division of French football.
As France's most successful club, they have won over 40 official honours,
including ten league titles and one major European trophy. Their home ground is
the Parc des Princes.
